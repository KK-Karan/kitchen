WELCOME!

A well-established company, where the heart of business is to project manage, design, produce and fit exclusive superior kitchen cabinets and built-in cupboards for newly built homes and projects, offering our clients personal hands-on services from design to installation. Where creative design and highly functional kitchens are the mixture of imagination and a sense of individuality and craftsmanship.

Our designs feature a signature, understated aesthetic, influenced by contemporary architecture and design, using innovative materials, texture and colour. Afri Kitchens designs are made to measure in a huge range of stunning finishes, expertly crafted by our skilled cabinet makers in our manufacturing facility in Silverton, Pretoria.
